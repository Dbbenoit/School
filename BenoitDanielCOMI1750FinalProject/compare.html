Provider Comparison

One of the most recognized brands in the webhosting space is GoDaddy.com. Go Daddy has been in buisness for over 20 years, has over 6000 employees, and has had over 20 million clients with over 80 million domains, making them the worlds largest domain registrar. GoDaddy claims to have extensive services that extend beyond webhosting, marketing themselves as a buisness resource to help their customers with many aspects of their buisnes, from site creation, hosing, P.O.S. management and marketing.
GoDaddy offers domain registration staring at 99 cents for the first year for less-desirable domains, and the price goes up from there. There hosting portfolio can get pretty complicated, they offer hosting for wordpress, shared web hosting and VPS hosting. Pricing starts as little as 5.99/monmth for basic shared hosting, and can reach into the hundres of dollars for VPS hosting. All of their packages includ the domain, email hosting and SSL certificates, although some of these benefits become add-ons after the first year, depending on the package.

Hostinger.com is a Lithuanian based hosting company founded in 2004. Hostingers mission statement is to give anyone the opportunity to make an online presence with as little resistance as possible. As such, they seem to cater to smaller sites, like small buisnesses and blogs. They have pivoted in recent years to provide much of their website building assistance with A.I., but their core buisness is still hosting.
Hostinger offers both shared hosting and VPS hosting. Like GoDaddy, there are many packages available to try to appeal to a large market. Their "webhosting" packages seem to be geared toward people with little web experiance, as they all include A.I. site generation in the form of "vibe coding credits." The VPS options are more straigtforward. The user picks their CPU, RAM, storage and bandwidth. Prices are from 6.49 to 25.99 per month while under promotion, and 19.49 to 73.99 per month at regular price.

Dreamhost.com is the smallest host on this list. They have been in buisness since 1997 and have had about 400,000 clients in that time. Located in California, they seem to market themselves as a simple webhosting service. Their product offerings are more streamlines than the other two service providers, offering just three plans; a basic webhosting offering, a mangaged hosting option and a VPS buisness package. Prices start at under 3 dollars/month for the basic plan to 13.75/month for the buisness plan, although these are promotional pricing.
