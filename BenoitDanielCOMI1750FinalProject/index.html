Page Introduction

Welcome to my research project on web hosting

I've collected some of the important infomation for beginners looking to host their own webpage. Throughout the semster, we have been building our skills in creating beginner websites using HTML and CSS. Up until this point, we have just been storing the pages in folders and viewing them locally. This is fine for doing homework assignments and maybe some fun projects, but the world wide web is all about sharing ideas and to get these ideas out there, we need to post them online.

I welcome you to click the links above in the navigation bar. Each of the links hosts some introductory information about the topics lised in the titles. The first stop should be the Domain Names tab, it will help break down the pieces of the domain name that will help you chose the recognizable identity of your hosted site. From there, try out the Shard Hosting, Virtual Private Server, and Dedicated Hosting tabs. These can help you pick the kind of host provider thats best for your site. The Managed vs Unmanaged Hosting link brings you to a summary of the levels of assistance offered by different hosts and the SSL link teaches about the tiers of security used on modern pages. Finally, when you are looking for pricing, the Provider Comparison and Free Web Hosting hyperlinks can show you typical pricing avaiable.

The purpose of this site isn't to teach you how to create a website, more its my hope that this information can help guide students in the right direction with hosting their first webpage, and as an aside, I hope it also earns me a good grade, as this is also my final assignment for COMI 1750 at CCRI for Spring 2026.
In doing research for this assignment, I discovered that there are countless page hosts on the internet, all with different pricing structures and features. This colation of my findings doesn't explain individual pricing well enough to make any real recomendations and I encourage anyone looking to publish a site to spend some real time looking at all of the options available in the market.
